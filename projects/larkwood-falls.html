Project Overview

Unlike many mascot horror games—such as FNAF: Security Breach—where animatronics roam and chase you, eventually leading to a jumpscare and a restart with only minor changes between zones, Larkwood Falls offers a fresh, varied experience.

Each zone introduces unique mechanics tied to its mascot:

  • In Benny’s Workshop, Benny roams and chases you with his hammer, leading to a jumpscare if he catches you.
  • In Jungle Junction, Gavin moves differently—crawling, swinging, and using his brute strength—making gameplay feel distinct and dynamic.
  • In Polar Plaza, Penny glides and slides over ice, uses swimming and diving abilities, and listens closely to every sound, creating tense and unpredictable encounters unlike anything in other mascot horror games.
Additionally, Larkwood Falls features upgradable tools. You collect Benny Tickets throughout the game, which can be spent at secured Upgrade Stations to enhance your equipment. This system adds depth, speeds up gameplay for achievements, and makes replaying the game more fun and engaging.

Key Features

  • Procedurally generated playpark environments
  • Unique mascot designs with different behaviors
  • Dynamic lighting and atmospheric effects
