X-Pool

X-Pool is a bold, culturally rich multiplayer pool game that blends classic billiards gameplay with modern live features. Players around the world can challenge friends or join global matchmaking for quick matches, compete in rotating tournaments and climb leaderboards, and unlock and collect unique cosmetics like cue skins and table designs. With optional Web3-powered ownership and token-driven community events, X-Pool offers a player-first experience that balances competitive thrills with social engagement and rewarding progress.
